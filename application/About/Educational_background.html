La Trinidad Academy

I started my education at La Trinidad Academy, where I attended preschool. It was a time filled with curiosity, learning through play, and building early social skills. This stage helped me become more confident and prepared me for formal schooling. It played an important role in shaping my mindset toward education and my eagerness to keep exploring new things.

Jesus Good Shepherd School

I studied at Jesus Good Shepherd School for grade school, where I built a strong academic foundation. During these years, I developed good study habits and discovered my interest in logic and creativity. I also learned the value of discipline and responsibility, both in and out of the classroom—lessons that helped guide me into higher education.

De La Salle University - Dasmariñas

From Junior High to College, I studied at De La Salle University - Dasmariñas. I took the CSS track in Junior High and the STEM strand in Senior High. I graduated in July 2025 with a BS in Information Technology, major in Web Development. These years shaped my skills in tech, programming, and problem-solving—preparing me for a career in web development.
